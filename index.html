PROJECT DESCRIPTION STREET GENERATION PLOT ASSIGNMENT VISUALIZATION

PROJECT: CITY DRAFT

Aim and Context:

Our overarching aim is to create a rapid draft generator for user-defined urban development areas.

This tool opens up the possibility of prototyping very experimental urban planning strategies fast and efficiently. In this context, the aforementioned
algorithm demonstrates potentials and influences of different prerequisites like population density, amount of greenspaces, street layouts and much more.

Especially in the context of current challenges in urban planning like urban transformation, the current restructuring of car-based infrastructure,
growing inequality among residents of urban areas, but also during an emerging stream of new ideas and techniques in this field, this tool can help
to visualize and compare different scenarios with differing social and cultural base parameters and requirements.
In addition, it can help planners to interconnect parameters and requirements that might be hard to identify and measure in early planning stages.
While this tool certainly isn't meant to produce finished plans, it can be used by urban planners to build upon and be used as an initiator for
digitally informed urban designs.

Last but not least, in an age of a growing interest in participatory planning and a broader inclusion of the general public in urban planning,
due to its low entry threshold, this tool can provide a valuable resource to visualize different concepts of urban planning and to let people otherwise
not affiliated with this topic get involved in the early planning stages of projects, while playing with an experimental, but realistic environment.
